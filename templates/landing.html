GET STARTED

I'm new to IntelIntern. What is it and how can I get started?

IntelIntern is a hybrid internship recommender system designed to connect students with exciting internship opportunities. We use a combination of your profile information and data collected from Glassdoor to suggest internships that perfectly match your interests and qualifications. Getting started is easy! Simply create an account on our platform. This will allow you to customize your profile, showcase your skills and interests, and unlock a world of personalized internship recommendations.

POWERED BY GLASSDOOR

How does IntelIntern use Glassdoor data to recommend internships?

IntelIntern goes beyond just your profile! We integrate data from Glassdoor, a leading company review platform, to provide a more comprehensive picture of internship opportunities. This allows us to recommend internships that not only align with your skills but also offer a positive work environment and culture, as reflected in Glassdoor employee reviews. For even more detailed information on companies you're interested in, you can visit Glassdoor directly.
